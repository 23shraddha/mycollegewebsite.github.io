Admission Process and Application


All applicants must submit the following documents. If you are shortlisted for any course, the interview panel will not proceed without these documents.



  1. Original Statement and attested photo copy of marks of 10th; I PUC / Class 11th or II PUC/Class 12th.
  2. Applicants who belong to SC/ST/Any other category and have indicated the same in the application form are required to produce supporting documents. This is mandatory.
  3. A document showing addresses proof of parent/local guardian.
  4. Transfer Certificates from the institution last studied (if available).
  5. Migration Certificate if the candidate is from a board other than Pre University Board of Karnataka or from an university other than Karnataka university (If available).
  6. Foreign candidates need to bring along a copy of passport and visa page.

  7. A copy of Adhara card.
  8. 06 passport size photographs.

Admissions are Open for 1st PUC Students.
